Hey, I'm Anshuman Uchil. I’m a video and interaction artist. But above all, I’m a storyteller.

I design playful installations, make music videos, create cartoons and animations, program web interactions, and more.

Most of the work I do is user-centric.
I write stories that I wish to tell people, and convey it to them through my work.

Speculative design or projection-mapped installations.

I wish to tell stories and have them be experienced in a way that above all else, inspires an emotional response. I want people to laugh, cry, feel loneliness, and I craft these emotions in a way that is meaningful through experience. Frequently experimenting with multimedia, I explore the extents of what storytelling can be.

Interactive Novels, Music reactive rooms.

By creating half-narratives with my own experiences, I invite you to fill in the other half with yours.
